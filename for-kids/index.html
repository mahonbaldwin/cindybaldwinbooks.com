When Cindy was 12, her favorite series was The Chronicles of Prydain by Lloyd Alexander. There are references to this series in two of Cindy's books. In The Stars of Whistling Ridge, Ravi's dad loves to experiment with new ice cream flavors. This is based on Cindy's husband, who loves to try new and unusual recipes for ice cream.
